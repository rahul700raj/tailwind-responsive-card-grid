## GitHub Pages Setup

To get your live project link, follow these steps:

### Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/rahul700raj/tailwind-responsive-card-grid
2. Click on **Settings** (top right)
3. Scroll down to **Pages** in the left sidebar
4. Under **Source**, select:
   - Branch: `main`
   - Folder: `/ (root)`
5. Click **Save**

### Step 2: Wait for Deployment

GitHub will automatically deploy your site. This takes 1-2 minutes.

### Step 3: Access Your Live Site

Your live project will be available at:

**üåê https://rahul700raj.github.io/tailwind-responsive-card-grid/**

- **Basic Version**: https://rahul700raj.github.io/tailwind-responsive-card-grid/
- **Intermediate Version**: https://rahul700raj.github.io/tailwind-responsive-card-grid/intermediate.html
